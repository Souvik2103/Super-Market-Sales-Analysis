This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of a supermarket's performance over a two-year period (2019-2020). The goal of this project is to track key performance indicators (KPIs), understand consumer behavior across different segments, and identify year-over-year (YoY) growth trends in sales and profit.

Based on the data visualized:

Overall Performance: Achieved total sales of $2M with a net profit of $175K across 22K orders.

Shipping Efficiency: The average shipping time is maintained at 4 days.

Preferred Payment Modes: Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most popular payment method (43%), followed by Online payments (35%).

Regional Dominance: The West Region contributes the highest sales volume (33%), while the South region shows the most room for growth.

Customer Segments: The Consumer segment is the primary driver of revenue, accounting for 48% of total sales.

Product Performance: Office Supplies is the leading category by volume, while Phones and Chairs are the top-selling sub-categories.
